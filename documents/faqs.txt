# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## General Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book?
A: We recommend booking at least 30 days in advance to get the best availability and pricing. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

### Q: Can I change my booking date?
A: Yes, you can reschedule your event free of charge if requested at least 30 days before the original date. Contact our booking team for availability.

### Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: We offer flexible cancellation terms. Cancellations more than 30 days before the event receive a full refund minus processing fees. See our full cancellation policy in the booking policy document.

## Venue and Facilities

### Q: What is the maximum capacity of your venues?
A: Our basic venue accommodates up to 50 guests, professional venue up to 200 guests, and premium venue up to 500 guests. We have multiple venues available.

### Q: Is parking available?
A: Yes, all our packages include parking. Basic includes 20 spots, Professional includes 50 spots, and Premium includes 100+ spots. Valet parking is available upon request.

### Q: Can we bring our own decorations?
A: Yes, you're welcome to bring your own decorations or use our decoration service. We charge $200-$1,000 for our decoration packages depending on the complexity.

### Q: Is WiFi available at the venue?
A: Yes, high-speed WiFi is available at all venues. Premium packages include dedicated bandwidth for streaming and large file transfers.

## Services and Support

### Q: Can I upgrade my package during the event?
A: Upgrades can be made with additional charges. Contact our on-site coordinator for details. Downgrades are not allowed but payment adjustments apply.

### Q: Do you provide event planning services?
A: Yes, event planning is included with our Corporate Package. For other packages, event planning is available for $300.

### Q: What if I need technical support during the event?
A: All packages include on-site technical support from our team. For extended support beyond event hours, please contact us in advance.

## Payments and Billing

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and checks with a 5% processing fee.

### Q: When is payment due?
A: 30% advance payment is required at booking confirmation. The remaining 70% is due 7 days before the event.

### Q: Can I get an invoice for corporate purposes?
A: Yes, detailed invoices are provided upon request. Contact our billing department for tax documentation or special billing arrangements.

### Q: Do you offer discounts?
A: Yes! We offer:
- 10% discount for bookings 60+ days in advance
- 15% discount for weekday bookings (Monday-Thursday)
- 10% discount for bulk bookings (multiple events in a year)
- 20% discount for student organizations

## Catering and Refreshments

### Q: Can we bring our own catering?
A: We recommend using our approved caterers, but outside catering is permitted with prior approval. A 10% venue fee applies for outside catering.

### Q: Are beverages included in catering packages?
A: Basic catering includes water and coffee. Enhanced packages include soft drinks and tea. Alcoholic beverages are available for an additional charge.

## Special Requests

### Q: Can we host a wedding at your venue?
A: Yes, our premium venues are perfect for weddings. We offer customized wedding packages starting at $3,500.

### Q: Do you accommodate events with special needs?
A: Yes, we can arrange accessibility accommodations, dietary requirements, and special setups. Please inform us during the booking process.

### Q: Can we live-stream our event?
A: Yes, live streaming is available for $200 per event. Premium and Corporate packages include streaming capabilities.
