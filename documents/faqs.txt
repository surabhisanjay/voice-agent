Breakout® – FAQ & Knowledge Base
 
GENERAL
Q1. What is Breakout?
Breakout® is a real-life escape room experience where teams solve puzzles and follow a storyline inside a themed room within a fixed time.
Q2. What is an escape room?
An escape room is a team game where players enter a themed room, find clues, solve puzzles, and complete a mission before time runs out.
Q3. Are players actually locked inside?
No. Players are not locked in. Staff monitor the game and assist if needed.
 
LOCATIONS
Q4. Where is Breakout located?

GAME DURATION & ARRIVAL
Q5. How long does a game last?
Each game runs for around 50 minutes.
Q6. How early should we arrive?
Players should arrive 20 minutes before the slot time.
 
BOOKING
Q7. Is advance booking required?
Yes. All bookings are done online.
Q8. Can I walk in without booking?
Walk-ins are allowed only if slots are available. Slots cannot be held without advance payment.
Q9. Can I book by phone?
If you cannot book online, staff can assist by collecting details and sending a payment link.

PAYMENTS
Q32. Is advance payment required?
Yes. Payment must be completed within 15 minutes of receiving the link.
Q33. Can I pay at the venue?

LATE ARRIVAL
Q34. What if we are late?
The game starts at the scheduled time. Late arrival reduces game time.
