# Company Booking Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the booking procedures, requirements, and policies for our event and service booking system.

## Required Documents for Booking

### Primary Documents
1. **Valid ID Proof**: Government-issued identification such as passport, driver's license, or national ID card
2. **Booking Form**: Completed and signed booking form with all required details
3. **Advance Payment Receipt**: Proof of advance payment (typically 30% of total amount)
4. **Event Details**: Comprehensive information about the event including date, time, location, and number of attendees

### Supporting Documents (if applicable)
- Company registration documents (for corporate bookings)
- Authorization letter (for third-party bookings)
- Special requirements documentation (dietary, accessibility, etc.)

## Booking Process

### Step 1: Initial Inquiry
Contact our booking team with your event requirements. We will provide you with a quotation and availability confirmation.

### Step 2: Booking Form Submission
Submit the completed booking form along with all required documents.

### Step 3: Advance Payment
Pay the 30% advance payment as per the quotation. This amount is non-refundable but can be adjusted towards your final invoice.

### Step 4: Confirmation
Once payment is received and documents are verified, you will receive a booking confirmation email with all details.

### Step 5: Final Payment
Pay the remaining 70% of the amount at least 7 days before the event.

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancellation more than 30 days before the event: Full refund of advance payment
- Cancellation 15-30 days before the event: 50% refund of advance payment
- Cancellation less than 15 days before the event: No refund

## Rescheduling

You can reschedule your event free of charge if requested at least 30 days before the original date.

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- Bank Transfer
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Mobile Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Check (with 5% additional processing fee)
