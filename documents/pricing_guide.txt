# Pricing Guide

## Service Packages

### Basic Package - $500
- Suitable for small gatherings (up to 50 guests)
- Standard venue setup
- Basic audio-visual equipment
- 4 hours of service

**Includes:**
- Venue rental
- Basic tables and chairs
- Standard lighting
- Single microphone
- Parking for up to 20 vehicles

### Professional Package - $1,500
- Suitable for medium events (50-200 guests)
- Enhanced venue setup
- Professional audio-visual equipment
- 6 hours of service

**Includes:**
- Premium venue rental
- Customizable tables and chairs
- Professional lighting system
- 4 wireless microphones
- Video projection setup
- Parking for up to 50 vehicles

### Premium Package - $3,500
- Suitable for large events (200-500 guests)
- Full customization available
- State-of-the-art audio-visual equipment
- 8 hours of service

**Includes:**
- Premium venue rental with dedicated coordinator
- Full customization of furniture and layout
- Professional lighting and effects
- 8 wireless microphones and mixing console
- 4K video projection and recording
- Valet parking for 100+ vehicles
- On-site catering coordination

### Corporate Package - $5,000+
- Custom designed for corporate events
- Flexible duration
- Advanced A/V and technology

**Includes:**
- All Premium Package features
- Dedicated event manager
- 24/7 support
- Custom branding integration
- Video streaming capabilities
- Conference room setup

## Add-on Services

- Catering Service: $25 per person (minimum 20 people)
- Event Photography: $300 (4 hours), $500 (8 hours)
- Video Recording: $400 (4 hours), $700 (8 hours)
- Live Streaming: $200 per event
- DJ Service: $400 (4 hours), $600 (8 hours)
- Decorations: $200 - $1,000 (based on requirements)
- Security Staff: $50 per person per hour
- Event Planning: $300 (included with Corporate Package)

## Discount Policy

- **Advance Booking Discount**: 10% off if booked 60+ days in advance
- **Off-Peak Discount**: 15% off for weekday bookings (Monday-Thursday)
- **Bulk Booking Discount**: 10% off when booking multiple events in a year
- **Student Organization Discount**: 20% off (with valid organization documentation)

## Payment Terms

- 30% advance payment required at booking confirmation
- 70% final payment due 7 days before the event
- Late payment penalty: 5% of total amount per week
- All prices are inclusive of taxes

## Refund Policy

Refunds are processed based on the cancellation date:
- 30+ days before: Full refund minus 10% processing fee
- 15-30 days: 50% refund
- < 15 days: No refund (amount can be credited for future bookings)
